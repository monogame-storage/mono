# GRAVITON

## Title
GRAVITON: Gravity Puzzle

## Short Description
Rotate gravity to solve falling-block puzzles. A fresh twist on a classic.

## Long Description
GRAVITON reinvents the falling-block puzzle by putting gravity itself in your hands. Rotate the entire world to change which way blocks fall, transforming familiar stacking gameplay into a mind-bending spatial challenge. Plan your moves carefully -- a single gravity shift can cascade blocks in unexpected ways, clearing lines you never thought possible.

The game features a clean, minimalist grayscale aesthetic that keeps your focus on the puzzle. As you progress, new block shapes and faster pacing demand sharper instincts and deeper strategic thinking. Every stage tests your ability to visualize how pieces will settle after a rotation, rewarding players who think several moves ahead.

Whether you are a seasoned puzzle veteran looking for a new challenge or a newcomer drawn in by its elegant simplicity, GRAVITON offers a satisfying loop of tension and release. Master gravity rotation, chase high scores, and discover that sometimes the best move is to flip the whole world upside down.

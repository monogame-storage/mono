# SHADOW LEAP

## Title
Shadow Leap

## Short Description
Master wall jumps and air dashes across 5 hand-crafted grayscale levels.

## Long Description
Shadow Leap is a precision platformer that puts your reflexes to the ultimate test. Take control of a nimble silhouette as you wall jump, air dash, and leap through five meticulously hand-crafted levels. Every surface is a launchpad, every gap a challenge, and every landing a triumph.

Built around tight, responsive controls, Shadow Leap features coyote time for forgiving ledge jumps, a powerful air dash for crossing deadly gaps, and fluid wall jumping that lets you scale vertical surfaces with style. The mechanics are simple to learn but demand mastery to conquer each stage.

Wrapped in a striking retro grayscale aesthetic, the game strips away distractions and focuses on what matters most: pure platforming skill. Each of the five levels introduces new obstacles and combinations that will push your precision to the limit.

Whether you are a seasoned speedrunner chasing flawless runs or a newcomer looking for a satisfying challenge, Shadow Leap delivers tight action in a compact, replayable package. No filler, no clutter — just you, your shadow, and the leap ahead.

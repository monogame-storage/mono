# NITRO DASH

## Title
Nitro Dash

## Short Description
Pseudo-3D arcade racer with nitro boost, checkpoints, and traffic dodging.

## Long Description
Nitro Dash is a retro-inspired pseudo-3D arcade racing game in the spirit of classic OutRun. Grip the wheel, hit the gas, and blast down an endless highway at breakneck speed. Weave through oncoming traffic, nail every checkpoint before the timer runs out, and hammer the nitro button to leave everything behind in a blur of exhaust and adrenaline.

The road stretches toward the horizon with smooth sprite-scaling visuals that capture the look and feel of 1980s arcade cabinets. Each stage brings tighter traffic, sharper turns, and shorter checkpoint windows. Strategic use of your limited nitro supply is the difference between crossing the finish line and watching the clock hit zero.

Pick your lane, time your boosts, and chase the high score. Nitro Dash delivers pure arcade tension in a compact, retro-styled package -- no loot boxes, no tutorials, just raw speed and split-second reflexes.

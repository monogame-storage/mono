# MONO BEAT

## Title
MONO BEAT

## Short Description
A 4-lane rhythm game with timing judgments, combos, and letter grades.

## Long Description
MONO BEAT is a fast-paced 4-lane rhythm game that puts your timing and reflexes to the test. Notes cascade down the screen in sync with the music, and your job is to hit them with pinpoint accuracy. Every input is graded with precise timing judgments ranging from Perfect to Miss, rewarding players who can stay locked in the groove.

The game features three handcrafted songs, each offering a distinct challenge. Whether you are warming up with a mellow opener or pushing through a frantic closer, MONO BEAT keeps you engaged with escalating patterns and rhythmic complexity. A real-time combo counter tracks your consecutive hits, driving you to maintain focus and chase longer streaks.

Your performance is evaluated at the end of each song with a letter grade from S down to D, giving you a clear target to beat on your next attempt. The scoring system balances accuracy and consistency, so every Perfect matters and every Miss stings. Three difficulty levels let newcomers ease in while veterans chase flawless runs.

Rendered in a clean retro monochrome aesthetic, MONO BEAT strips away distractions and lets the rhythm take center stage. No flashy colors, no clutter, just you, four lanes, and the beat.

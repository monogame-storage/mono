# GREY BASTION

## Title
Grey Bastion

## Short Description
Command four unique towers to survive 20 waves of relentless assault.

## Long Description
Grey Bastion is a tower defense game built on strategic depth and careful planning. Place and upgrade four distinct tower types -- Arrow, Cannon, Magic, and Frost -- each with unique strengths, ranges, and upgrade paths. Every placement decision matters as enemy waves grow fiercer and more varied across all 20 stages.

The Arrow Tower offers rapid, precise strikes against single targets. The Cannon Tower delivers devastating area damage to clustered foes. The Magic Tower bypasses armor with ethereal bolts, and the Frost Tower slows advancing enemies to a crawl, buying precious time for your other defenses. Upgrading each tower unlocks improved stats and new abilities, letting you tailor your bastion to the threats ahead.

With a winding battlefield and limited resources, victory demands more than reflexes -- it demands strategy. Study enemy wave compositions, invest upgrades wisely, and exploit chokepoints to hold the line. The monochrome retro aesthetic strips away distractions, putting pure tactical thinking at the center of every session.

Twenty waves stand between you and the defence of your stronghold. Build your bastion. Hold the grey.

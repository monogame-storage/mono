# VOID STORM

## Title
Void Storm

## Short Description
Vertical shmup with boss fights, power-ups, and a relentless combo system.

## Long Description
Void Storm drops you into a relentless vertical gauntlet where five distinct enemy types swarm from every angle. Small drones rush in tight formations, armored fighters strafe across the screen, heavy cruisers absorb punishment while returning fire, cloaked mines drift silently into your path, and elite interceptors mirror your movements with lethal precision. Every wave demands a different strategy, and hesitation is fatal.

Each stage culminates in a multi-phase boss encounter. Bosses shift attack patterns as their armor breaks away, revealing new weak points and deadlier weapons. Learn their tells, dodge walls of grayscale ordnance, and strike hard during the narrow openings between barrages.

Power-ups cascade from destroyed enemies: spread shots, piercing beams, shield charges, and the devastating void lance that tears through entire formations. Choosing which upgrades to grab and which to skip is a split-second decision that shapes every run.

Chain your kills to build the combo multiplier. Keep the counter climbing and the screen fills with score bonuses, but one missed beat resets everything. The combo system rewards aggressive play and flawless movement, turning each run into a high-score chase wrapped in pure retro grayscale intensity.

# KNOCKOUT!

## Title
KNOCKOUT!

## Short Description
Step into the ring and fight through 4 opponents in this retro boxing tournament.

## Long Description
KNOCKOUT! is a pixel-art boxing game that puts you toe-to-toe with four distinct opponents, each with their own fighting style and patterns. Learn their tells, dodge their attacks, and unleash devastating combos to climb the tournament bracket and claim the championship belt.

Master the combo system to chain jabs, hooks, and uppercuts into powerful sequences that break through your opponent's guard. But watch your stamina bar closely — every punch thrown and every hit absorbed drains your fighter's energy. Overextend yourself and you'll be left wide open for a counterpunch that could end the fight.

Each of the four opponents presents a unique challenge. From the lightning-fast jab specialist in Round 1 to the heavy-hitting slugger waiting in the championship bout, you'll need to adapt your strategy for every fight. Time your blocks, manage your stamina, and look for the opening to land that one decisive blow.

With tight retro-style controls, satisfying K.O. mechanics, and a tournament mode that keeps the stakes rising, KNOCKOUT! delivers pure arcade boxing action one round at a time.

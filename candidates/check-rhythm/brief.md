# CHECK RHYTHM

## Concept
Chess attack patterns become rhythm beats. Sequences of chess moves scroll toward a hit zone — the player must press the matching direction at the right moment. "Check!" appears on beat requiring a dodge input. Patterns escalate: knight forks become syncopated rhythms, discovered checks become surprise beats, double checks require two rapid inputs. The music IS chess.

## Gameplay
- Beats scroll from right to left toward a strike zone on the left
- Each beat corresponds to a chess attack direction (up/down/left/right)
- Player presses the matching direction when the beat hits the strike zone
- Perfect/Good/Miss timing windows determine score
- Combos multiply score; misses break combos
- Patterns progress through chess themes: pawn advances, knight forks, bishop diagonals, rook files, queen sweeps, king checkmates

## Pattern Types
- **Pawn March**: Steady quarter-note up beats
- **Knight Fork**: Syncopated L-shaped pairs (up+right, down+left)
- **Bishop Slide**: Diagonal sequences (alternating up/down with left/right)
- **Rook Charge**: Rapid same-direction runs
- **Queen Sweep**: All four directions in quick succession
- **Double Check**: Two simultaneous inputs required

## Visual Style
1-bit monochrome. Chess piece silhouettes mark beats. The board scrolls as a grid backdrop. Strike zone pulses with the rhythm. Screen flashes on perfect hits. "CHECK!" text flashes on special beats.

## Sound Design
Rich layered audio: bass rhythm on channel 0, melody on channel 1, percussion on channel 2, SFX on channel 3. Musical phrases correspond to chess patterns. Perfect hits produce satisfying chord stabs. Misses create dissonant buzzes.

## Demo Mode
After idle on title, auto-plays with a clock overlay. AI hits beats automatically with visual feedback. Returns to title on any input.

## Controls
- D-pad: Match beat directions
- A/Start: Confirm / Pause
- Touch: Tap quadrants for directions

# GRAVITY BALL

A physics playground where tilting the device changes gravity direction. Navigate a ball through obstacle courses, collect stars, and avoid spikes. Gravity can pull in any direction -- up, down, left, right, or diagonal -- based on device tilt.

## Controls
- **Tilt device**: Change gravity direction (uses motion API with d-pad fallback)
- **A button**: Jump / restart after game over
- **Start**: Pause / unpause

## Gameplay
- Ball falls in the direction of gravity (determined by tilt)
- Collect all stars in each level to advance
- Avoid red spikes -- touching them costs a life
- 3 lives per game, earn bonus lives every 3 levels
- 10 levels of increasing difficulty with walls, platforms, and moving obstacles
- Demo mode plays automatically after idle on title screen
